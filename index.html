Him & Her. Their Happy Story!

Ravnoor Kaur (ritika)

Bride

She was raised with inheritance of humble nature and a tranquil personality & she relied on her parents to choose her life partner. While he's the man who sees the world differently and like to take things under his control, so it was no one else but himself who started the quest for true soul mate. The Queen's parents guided her to look for her better half through matrimonial sites, and hallelujah, the king was already there. So, here goes the story. They met through Shaadi.com . So, it was the first rendezvous at her place. He came along with the family , there were no butterflies in the stomach at time yet some flying elephants of thoughts in the heads. But him with his affectionate and neighbourly personality overwhelmed her and they just couldn't stop talking once they started.

Now, it was time for something official, and the first step was taken and their knot was tied with "Roka" ceremony in front of friends and family.

Her words "His family, just like him, was very supporting and welcoming. Not even for one second I felt that I've met them for the first time. The second time we met, was just the two of us. We talked for very long hours, we both wished it to last for eternity ^_^. I felt so comfortable with him and thus I realized that this is the one !! Man of my life :D"

His words "She's of-course very charming and beautiful. On our first meeting I was little nervous and cautious, but it was all matter of few seconds she was so cool and I got so comfortable that we kept on talking and talking and we almost lost track of time. That day I decided to make her mine for the rest of my life."

Anand Karaj

Date:

31st of January 2015

Time:

1:00 in the afternoon

Location:

Gurudwara Singh Sabha, Kirti Nagar

Anand Karaj is the Sikh marriage ceremony, meaning "Blissful Union" or "Joyful Union", that was introduced by Guru Amar Das. The four Lavan were composed by his successor, Guru Ram Das. As, Anand Karaj is the most important ritual in Sikhism, the couple will start its journey together with blessings from the GOD and according to Sikh Reht Maryada (Sikh code of conduct and conventions). This religious ceremony will take place in the presence of Guru Granth Sahib at Gurudwara Singh Sabha, Kirti Nagar. You're humbly requested to reach at the place and give the couple blessings for a happy and prosperous life.
